Company Overview  
 

Bhavya pipes is a company, lead by Mr. M. Dinesh Bhandari Jain having field experience of 24 years, is dedicated to provide the best and quality products in PLASTIC PIPING PRODUCTS and a durable collection of Water Tanks.

The quality products are procured from well known companies like “SUPREME”, “MFLEX”, “PRINCE” & “ASHIRVAD” brand UPVC Pipes & Fittings and “AQUATECH”, “INFRA” & “SINTEX” brand Plastic Water storage tanks. Moreover a personalised attention to the requirement of each client is given. We provide one of the widest ranges of products for variety of piping requirement to satisfy allround customer needs. We have huge inventory of all ranges & varieties of Plastic piping system to cater the needs of our prospective buyers.
